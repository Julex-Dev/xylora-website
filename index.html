FAQ

Questions we hear a lot

Can't find your answer? Get in touch →

How long does a typical website project take?
+
Most website projects take between 2–6 weeks depending on complexity. A simple service website typically takes 2–3 weeks. A custom booking system or ecommerce integration will take closer to 4–6 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline in your proposal.
What industries do you work with?
+
We primarily work with small businesses and local service businesses: trades, gyms, yoga studios, consultants, coaches, health businesses, and startups. If you have an audience to reach and a service to deliver, we can almost certainly help.
Do I need to be technical to work with you?
+
Not at all. We handle everything technical and explain things in plain language. By the time your project is live, you'll know exactly how to use everything we've built — no coding knowledge required.
What does a website typically cost?
+
Website projects typically start from $2,500 for a simple service site and go up depending on complexity, the number of pages, and any integrated systems. AI and automation projects are scoped separately. All pricing is fixed — you'll always know the total cost upfront.
Will I own the website and all its content?
+
Yes, always. Once your project is paid for, everything belongs to you — the code, the content, the domain. There are no subscription fees or lock-in contracts for the website itself. Some third-party tools (hosting, booking platforms) have their own fees, which we'll always explain upfront.
Do you offer ongoing support?
+
Yes. We offer optional monthly maintenance plans that include updates, backups, security monitoring, and priority support. Many clients also engage us on a retainer for ongoing improvements and new features.

Real project

The challenge

Blue Moon Yoga had been in business for over 25 years but relied almost entirely on repeat customers and word-of-mouth. Their website was built in 2015 when the business was still small and hadn't been touched since. It loaded slowly, looked broken on mobile, and had no clear way for visitors to request a quote.

The owner spent significant time answering enquiry calls that could have been self-service — he was most likely regularly losing work to competitors with better-looking websites.

Is it really free, no strings attached?
+
Yes, completely. We produce a genuine audit report for every business that requests one. We don't gate it behind a sales call or require payment information. If you choose to work with us afterwards, that's great — but the audit has real value regardless.
Do I need to have a website already?
+
You don't need a website to benefit from an audit. If you don't have one, we'll review your current online presence — Google Business Profile, social media, and anything else — and recommend where to start.
How long does the audit take?
+

What happens after I receive the audit?
